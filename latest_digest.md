## [Sign of the future: GPT-5.5](https://www.oneusefulthing.org/p/sign-of-the-future-gpt-55)
*One Useful Thing — 2026-04-23*

**TLDR:** GPT-5.5 Pro demonstrates continued rapid AI improvement across models, applications, and tools, though some limitations in creative writing persist.

**Key points:**
- GPT-5.5 Pro completed a complex 3D harbor town simulation task in 20 minutes versus 33 minutes for GPT-5.4 Pro, and was the only model to actually simulate town evolution rather than just replacing buildings.
- The author generated a near-PhD-quality academic paper on crowdfunding from scattered data files using only four prompts with Codex (GPT-5.5), producing a real literature review and valid statistics.
- A new image model (GPT-imagegen-2) now renders high-quality text and detailed images reliably, enabling practical applications like creating PowerPoint slides and product mockups that were impossible months ago.
- GPT-5.5 successfully created a complete, illustrated, 101-page tabletop RPG with functional rules, though long-form creative fiction still shows characteristic weaknesses: repetitive ornate language, unrealistic dialogue, and uncanny phrasing.
