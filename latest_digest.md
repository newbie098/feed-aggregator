## [The making of a Frontier Firm: How AI is redesigning work at BNY](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/worklab/the-making-of-a-frontier-firm-how-ai-is-redesigning-work-at-bny)
*Microsoft WorkLab — 2026-05-12*

**TLDR:** BNY Mellon is training employees to design, govern, and implement AI safely within a heavily regulated banking environment.

**Key points:**
- BNY is building internal capability by teaching employees how to design and govern AI systems rather than relying solely on external vendors or consultants.
- The bank is implementing structured approaches to AI governance to ensure compliance with financial regulations while scaling AI deployment across operations.
- BNY is treating AI implementation as a workforce redesign effort, using "digital labor" to augment and transform existing job functions rather than simply automating them away.
